Welcome to Hao Sushi

Ottawa's #1 Sushi Restaurant!

Hello and welcome! We here at Hao Sushi are passionate about one thing: serving you the best quality sushi with the best possible service!

Relax with a pot of our green tea and listen to some traditional music while you wait for your order. We always use the freshest ingredients in our rolls, and our huge selection of menu items mean you can sample many different styles of Japanese and Asian cuisine. Our comfortable seating accomodations are complete with iPads at every table to make your ordering as quick and easy as possible.

Located in Nepean, we compete with some of the busiest sushi restaurants in the West End. Our All-You-Can-Eat restaurant is quaint and cozy for a more intimate dining experience.

Be sure to check out our menu to get an idea of all the yummy food you can experience here at Hao!
